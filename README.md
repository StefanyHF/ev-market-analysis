# EV Market Analysis (IEA Dataset)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes electric vehicle (EV) market trends using data from the International Energy Agency (IEA).  
It focuses on EV market share evolution across countries over time.

## Objective
To understand how EV adoption has evolved in major economies and compare growth patterns between countries.

## Dataset
- Source: International Energy Agency (IEA)
- Dataset: Global EV Data Explorer
- Metric used: EV sales share (% of total car market)

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

## Methodology
- Filtered EV sales share data
- Removed projection years
- Grouped data by country and year
- Compared trends across Germany, USA, and China
- Calculated market share growth over time

## Key Insights
- China shows the strongest transformation in EV adoption
- USA and Germany show slower but steady growth

## Output
The analysis includes line charts showing EV market share trends over time.
